Sympliact is a dynamic care management platform designed to enhance the experience of healthcare delivery. It facilitates real-time collaboration and connection between healthcare providers and patients. The platform aims to reduce time spent on low-value tasks and emphasizes efficient, high-value, personalized human care interactions. Sympliact is HIPAA-compliant and prioritizes the privacy and security of patient data.
