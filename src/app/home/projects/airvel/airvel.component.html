Airvel is a leading online platform in the private aviation industry, known for being the world's first true multi-sided booking engine for private jet charters. It connects aircraft management companies and air charter operators directly with customers, providing a seamless and efficient process for booking private flights. Airvel's system allows customers to easily search, compare, and book flights from a vast selection of over 4,000 pre-vetted private aircraft, streamlining the traditional process of chartering a private jet.
